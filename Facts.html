Tidbits of Grass-Fed, Free-Range Cattle

1. 100% of our grass-fed beef graze in our pasture year-round and are not fed a processed diet with hormones during their life time, which means we do not "bulk" them up quickly in order to deliver beef to your table.

2. Since they are not confined nor crowded to a localized area, the risk of contamination in their diet or having unhealthy cattle is low, which gives you the safest, healthiest, and most superior quality beef.

3. Grass-fed beef are leaner, which helps in lowering your cholesterol and caloric intake.

4. Grass-fed beef provide higher CLA (conjugated linoleic acid), which helps improve your immune function.

5. Grass-fed cows are richer in omega-3 fats, vitamin E, beta-carotene and many other vitamins; which strengthens your cardiovascular system.
