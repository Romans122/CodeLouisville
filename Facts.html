Some Facts on Grass-Fed, Free-Range Cattle

1. 100% of our grass-fed beef graze in our pasture year-round and are not fed a processed diet during their life time, which means we do not "bulk" them up quickly in order to deliver meat to your table.

2. Since they are not confined nor crowded to a localized area, there is a lower risk of contaminated feed and sick cattle.

3. Grass fed cows are richer in omega-3 fats, vitamin E, beta-carotene and many other vitamins.

4. Grass-fed beef are leaner and lower in cholesterol.

5. Grass-fed beef provide higher CLA (conjugated linoleic acid), which helps improve immune function.
