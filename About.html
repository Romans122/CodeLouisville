About Us

At Abba's Anoited Acres, our business practices are no different than how we live our personal lives. Our first priority is to God. We believe by putting Him first, then we will meet your needs in every way: long, lasting, caring relationships through superior customer service and a superior quality product you can trust.

We are a family-run ranch serving the local southern Indiana area. Our vision is that Abba's Anoited Acres is a recognized house-hold name and your one and only source for great, quality, and nutritional beef.

Our mission is to serve you with integrity, excellence, and love so that you know that Abba's Anoited Acres is truly a different business from others.

We sell our products in bulk, either in whole, half, or quarters. The beef is cut to your specifications through a local processing company. We service the southern Indiana area and will also deliver it to your door. If you are interested in purchasing, please contact us .
